About me

My name’s Jérémie Jaouen, I am a 18 years old student of the ERACOM (Ecole Romande D’arts et de Communication du Canton de Vaud) since two years, where I make an Interactive Media Designer apprenticeship. I am passionate by 3D animation and films making.

I started 3D learning on Blender since 3 years on my free time with following tutorials on YouTube. I quickly started to love doing 3D scene and talk with community. This discovery motivated me to do my studies in art school to improve myself. In this School, I learned to use a camera, some lights and above all multiple software. This is where I understood that I want to transform this activity into my profession.

I’m fascinated by pictures some artist can do and it motivated me to make my own.

My training included a six months internship, that's why I coded this website with the programming basics I acquired during my training and especially with a lot of tutorials.
